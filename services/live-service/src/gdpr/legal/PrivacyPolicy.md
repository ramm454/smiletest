### 1.2 Legal Basis for Processing
| Purpose | Legal Basis | Retention Period |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| Account Management | Contract (Article 6(1)(b)) | Until account deletion |
| Live Streaming | Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) | Session + 30 days |

| Payment Processing | Legal Obligation (Article 6(1)(c)) | 7 years (tax law) |
| Analytics | Legitimate Interest (Article 6(1)(f)) | 24 months anonymized |
| Marketing | Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) | Until withdrawal |
