Between: [Your Company] (Controller) and [Subprocessor] (Processor)

## 2. Processor Obligations
2.1 Process data only per documented instructions
2.2 Ensure confidentiality

Processor may engage subprocessors only with:
- Prior specific authorization
- Written agreement with same obligations
- Maintain list of subprocessors

- Access controls and logging
- Regular security testing
- Data minimization principles
- Privacy by design and default
