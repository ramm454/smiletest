Payment Terms & Conditions

1. Payment Processing

All payments are processed by third-party payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal, RazorPay). We do not store or process your payment card details on our servers.

2. Payment Gateway Responsibility

The payment gateway is responsible for:

  • Payment collection and processing
  • PCI-DSS compliance and security
  • Fraud detection and prevention
  • Chargeback and dispute handling
  • Payment data storage (encrypted)

3. Our Responsibility

We are responsible for:

  • Providing accurate billing information
  • Generating proper invoices and receipts
  • Fulfilling services/products upon payment
  • Customer support for billing inquiries
  • Tax calculation and reporting

4. Invoices

We provide invoices for all transactions for:

  • Your accounting records
  • Tax compliance purposes
  • Service/product documentation

5. Payment Issues

For payment-related issues (failed payments, refunds, disputes), contact:

  • Payment Gateway: For technical payment issues
  • Us: For service/product issues after payment

6. Data Privacy

We only share necessary information with payment gateways for transaction processing. Review the payment gateway's privacy policy for their data handling practices.

7. Contact Information

For billing inquiries: billing@yogaspa.com

For payment technical issues: Contact the respective payment gateway support
